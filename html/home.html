"tHe AlGoRiThM"®™

· Every post with a hashtag in it is posted to the front page. The front page shows the most recent X number of chronological posts.

· Posts without tags end up on the self page. To see the self page, click around a bit. You'll find it ðŸ˜‰

· When you make a post with a hashtag, the tags from it go in the cloud at the top. Clicking one of those tags brings you to a list of the last X chronological posts on that tag community. You can also just do something like https://lxmxnxl.com/t/ask to see the #ask tag community.

· Posts with a bajillion tags can get spammy, and so can repeated or empty posts, so those may get hung up somewhere, and aside from the first few tags, the rest go away.

Other details.

Everything you see on this site is ephemeral— a conversation in liminal space..

If a post can no longer be seen on the front page, or on a tag page, it's gone forever. There are no accounts, no profiles, no database, no hard drive, and everything is held in RAM. If there is a malfunction, a bug, an upgrade, or enough time passes, everything goes away, unable to be seen or retrieved.
